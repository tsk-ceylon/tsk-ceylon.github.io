## Layout Overview

> Layout: Portrait page — the most spatially busy in the brochure. Title "CONTACT INFORMATION" — "CONTACT" in navy and "INFORMATION" in red. Below the centered intro, the body is built from a grid of stacked cards:
>
> 1. **Top-left "GET IN TOUCH" card** — a vertical white card with 5 contact rows (Address, Phone, Email, Website, Follow Us) each with a circular navy icon, plus a row of 4 social-media icons at the bottom.
> 2. **Top-right hero card** — a photograph of the TSK Ceylon corporate building behind a grouping of red fire extinguishers and fire-safety devices.
> 3. **Bottom-left "WE SERVE ACROSS SRI LANKA" card** — a Sri Lanka silhouette map with red location pins + paragraph + small shield-flame icon.
> 4. **Center-bottom "WORKING HOURS" card** — navy header with clock icon + weekday/Saturday/Sunday hours + "24/7 EMERGENCY SUPPORT" red panel.
> 5. **Bottom-right "SCAN TO CONNECT" card** — instructions + QR code + website caption.
> 6. **Closing "THANK YOU FOR CHOOSING" rounded band** — navy text with handshake icon (left) and shield-flame icon (right) and 4 promise pillars beneath in a thin navy strip.
>
> Brand palette: navy `#0a1f44`, accent red `#c8102e`, white background.
> Recurring elements: page badge top-left, TSK Ceylon logo top-right, navy footer.
