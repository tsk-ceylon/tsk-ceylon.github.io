## Layout Overview

> Layout: Vertical, portrait-orientation cover. The top half is a clean white field anchored by a centered TSK Ceylon logo and the company name. The bottom half splits diagonally into a deep-navy panel on the left (tagline + contact details) and a photographic panel on the right (red fire extinguisher hero shot), separated by a sweeping red-and-navy curved divider.
> Brand palette: navy `#0a1f44`, accent red `#c8102e`, white background. Photo on the right adds neutral interior tones (cream wall, red fire-safety cabinets in the background).
> Recurring elements (introduced here): TSK Ceylon flame mark + wordmark; tagline "Safety Today, Secure Tomorrow"; three contact icons (phone, email, location); red-on-navy color pairing.
