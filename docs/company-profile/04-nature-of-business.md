## NATURE OF BUSINESS

**TSK CEYLON (PRIVATE) LIMITED** operates mainly in the field of fire protection and industrial safety solutions. We provide products and services designed to improve workplace and environmental safety.

> Layout: Vertical stack of 5 items, each row = circular navy icon on the left, bold red title and grey body text on the right, divided by a thin dotted rule.

#### 1. FIRE SAFETY EQUIPMENT SUPPLY

![Navy circular icon containing a white fire-extinguisher silhouette](placeholder)

We supply a wide range of fire extinguishers and related fire protection equipment suitable for homes, offices, factories, shops, warehouses, and industrial facilities.

#### 2. FIRE EXTINGUISHER SALES & REFILLING

![Navy circular icon containing a white extinguisher with a recycle/refill arrow](placeholder)

We provide both new fire extinguishers and professional refilling services to ensure that equipment remains functional and compliant with safety requirements.

#### 3. SAFETY SIGNAGE & STICKERS

![Navy circular icon containing a white warning-triangle with exclamation mark](placeholder)

We design and supply safety stickers, warning labels, and fire safety signage to help organizations maintain safety awareness and compliance.

#### 4. INDUSTRIAL & COMMERCIAL SAFETY SOLUTIONS

![Navy circular icon containing a white factory/industrial-building silhouette](placeholder)

We support commercial and industrial clients by supplying safety-related products and consultation services for fire protection requirements.

#### 5. INSTALLATION & MAINTENANCE SERVICES

![Navy circular icon containing crossed white wrench and screwdriver](placeholder)

Our company also assists customers with installation support and basic maintenance guidance for fire safety equipment.
